1. Grant of Rights

In Simple Terms:

This section means you are giving the person or company (the "Grantee") permission to use your image, voice, and name forever, anywhere in the world. This permission is permanent and you can't take it back. You also won't be paid for it.

I, [Full Name of Individual], residing at [Full Address], hereby grant to [Full Name of Organization or Individual] and its agents, successors, and assigns (the "Grantee") the perpetual, irrevocable, worldwide, and royalty-free right and license to use, reproduce, publish, distribute, and display my likeness, voice, name, and biographical information (collectively, the "Material"). This Material may be captured through photography, videography, or other forms of media.

2. Permitted Use

In Simple Terms:

The Grantee can use your likeness for almost anything, such as ads, social media, websites, articles, and training videos. They are also allowed to edit or change the photos/videos (e.g., crop, add filters) without asking for your approval first.

The Grantee may use the Material for any legitimate purpose, including, but not limited to:

  • Promotional and Marketing Use: The Material may be used in advertising, social media campaigns, brochures, websites, and other promotional materials.
  • Educational Use: The Material may be used in training videos, presentations, or educational publications.
  • Editorial Use: The Material may be used in articles, news reports, or other informational content.
  • Alterations and Modifications: I grant the Grantee the right to crop, edit, alter, composite, or otherwise modify the Material in any way they deem necessary. I waive any right to inspect or approve the finished product and release the Grantee from any claims arising from such alterations.

3. Release and Waiver of Claims

In Simple Terms:

You agree not to sue the Grantee for any reason related to the use of your likeness. This covers things like claims that the material makes you look bad (defamation) or that it invades your privacy.

I, for myself, my heirs, executors, and administrators, do hereby release, acquit, and forever discharge the Grantee from any and all claims, demands, or causes of action, whether known or unknown, which I have or may have in the future arising from the use of the Material. This release includes, but is not limited to, claims of defamation, invasion of privacy, publicity rights, or infringement of any other intellectual property rights.

4. Compensation

In Simple Terms:

You confirm that you understand you will not be paid any money for the use of your photos, videos, or voice.

I acknowledge that I will not receive any financial compensation or other remuneration for the Grantee's use of the Material.

5. Representations

In Simple Terms:

You are confirming that you are legally an adult (18 years or older) and have the right to sign this agreement. You also confirm that you have read and understood everything in this form.

I represent and warrant that I am at least eighteen (18) years of age and am fully authorized to enter into this agreement. I have read this form carefully, understand its contents, and voluntarily consent to all of its provisions.
